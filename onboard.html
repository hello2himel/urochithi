Sign Up

Create an account to get your own anonymous letterbox.

  1. Go to the Dashboard
  2. Click "Sign Up"
  3. Enter your name, email, and choose a password
  4. That's it — you're in!
✅ Free forever: No credit card required. Up to 60,000 monthly active users supported on the free tier.
Go to Dashboard →
2️⃣

Choose Your Username

After signing up, you'll be asked to pick a unique username. This becomes your letterbox address.

  • Use letters, numbers, hyphens, or underscores
  • Must be 3-30 characters
  • Choose wisely — this is what people will see!

Your letterbox URL will be:

urochithi.netlify.app/your-username
💡 Tip: Pick something short and memorable — it's what you'll share with people!
3️⃣

Share Your Link

Once you have your username, share your letterbox URL anywhere:

  • 📱 Add it to your Instagram / Twitter / TikTok bio
  • 💬 Share it in group chats
  • 📧 Include it in your email signature
  • 🔗 Post it anywhere you want to receive anonymous messages

Anyone who visits your link can send you an anonymous letter — no sign-up required for them!

📬 Check your letters: Visit the Dashboard anytime to read your anonymous letters, search through them, and manage your letterbox.

Import from Google Sheets

Coming from the old version? Bring your existing messages with you.

  1. Open your Google Sheet with the messages
  2. Go to File → Download → Comma-separated values (.csv)
  3. Log into your Dashboard
  4. Click the 📥 Import button
  5. Select your CSV file and click Import
💡 CSV Format: The importer automatically detects columns named "Timestamp", "Message", and "Session ID" (case-insensitive). As long as your CSV has at least a "Message" column, the import will work.

You can also export your data anytime using the 📤 Export button in the dashboard.
